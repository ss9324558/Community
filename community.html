We are a friendly creative web developer community (called Devkode) of passionate front-end developers.
Our goal is to connect developers of all stripes and aid in continued growth in their careers and the pursuit of knowledge.
We'd love to have you, whether you are looking to connect with like-minded devs, learn from others and ask questions or share your work and receive feedback.

Bootcamps

We are organising tech bootcamps randomly throughout the year where we teach specific skills in areas of UI such as Web performances, System Designs etc. These bootcamps vary somewhere between 3hrs to 7hrs according to the topics being covered.
